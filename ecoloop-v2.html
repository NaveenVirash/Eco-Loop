How EcoLoop works

Four steps to turn your unwanted household items into someone else's treasure — or responsibly recycle them.

01
📸
Post your item
List any household item — furniture, clothes, kitchenware, electronics. Add photos, describe the condition, set free or a price.
⭐ +50 pts for free listing
02
🔍
Someone finds it
Community members browse, filter by category and city, contact you directly, and arrange a local collection.
⭐ +20 pts when claimed
03
♻️
Send to recycler
Items that nobody claims? Schedule a pickup with one of our certified recycling partners. They handle proper disposal.
⭐ +15 pts per recycled item
04
🏆
Earn & climb
Every action earns EcoPoints. Collect badges, maintain streaks, and climb the Top Fans leaderboard.
⭐ Unlock Top Fan status
